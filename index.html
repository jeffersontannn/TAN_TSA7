Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

Miles Morales: is the central protagonist of Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse. He starts as a regular teenager in Brooklyn who gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. Initially struggling with his newfound powers, he receives guidance from alternate universe versions of Spider-Man, including Peter Parker and Gwen Stacy. Throughout the film, Miles learns to embrace his role as Spider-Man and becomes a key figure in saving his reality from a dimensional threat. Miles brings a fresh perspective to the Spider-Man legacy with his unique background and abilities, making him a relatable and inspiring character. Peter Parker: In Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse, Peter Parker is depicted as an experienced Spider-Man from an alternate dimension. He serves as a mentor to Miles Morales, guiding him in embracing his new role as Spider-Man. This version of Peter Parker is slightly older and more world-weary, having faced numerous challenges in his crime-fighting career. Gwen Stacy: Gwen Stacy, also known as Spider-Gwen or Ghost-Spider, is another dimension's version of Spider-Woman. In Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse, she is a confident and capable hero who teams up with other Spider-People to defeat a common threat. Gwen is depicted as skilled in combat and highly intelligent, adding a unique dynamic to the team of Spider-Heroes. These characters bring depth and diversity to the Spider-Verse storyline, each with their own unique abilities and personalities.

About the Movie

"Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" is a 2018 American animated superhero film featuring the Marvel Comics character Miles Morales / Spider-Man, produced by Columbia Pictures and Sony Pictures Animation in association with Marvel, and distributed by Sony Pictures Releasing.
